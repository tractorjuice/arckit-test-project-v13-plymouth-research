## What This Data Reveals

### 1. Scale Varies Dramatically
- From £1 net assets (chains with group accounting) to £1.6M (Stoke Village Cafe)
- Employee count ranges from 0 (consolidated) to 2,411 (BrewDog full company)

### 2. Micro-Entity Exemptions Limit Data
- Only 1 restaurant disclosed turnover
- Only 3 disclosed profit/loss
- Most qualify for micro-entity exemption (<£632k turnover)

### 3. Chain Restaurants Have Different Accounting
- Large chains (Greggs, KFC, Nando's) file consolidated accounts
- Plymouth branches don't report separate financials
- Often show £1-£2 net assets (nominal values)

### 4. Independent Restaurants More Transparent
- Better disclosure rates for local businesses
- More complete balance sheet data
- Employee counts available for most
